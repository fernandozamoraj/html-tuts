Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flower
  • …more stuff…

Directions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees F

  2. Mix butter, sugar, flower, vanilla and more stuff in a big bowl.

  3. Bake for about 10 minutes in the oven, or until edges are nicely browned.

  4. Remove from oven and allow to cool for about five minutes
